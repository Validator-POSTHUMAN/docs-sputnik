# First Web2 sign up

At the first **Web2 connection**, [**Sputnik App**](./) checks the [**Internal Record**](../glossary-of-terms-and-scheme/internal-record.md):&#x20;
* If in the Internal Record exist connection between Username and Address - Sputnik App shows available balance of address to User 
* If the [**Internal Record**](../glossary-of-terms-and-scheme/internal-record.md) does not contain information about usernames and/or email, [**Sputnik App**](./) creates an [**Internal Address**](../glossary-of-terms-and-scheme/internal-address.md) for the [**User**](../glossary-of-terms-and-scheme/user.md), encrypt Username and/or Email, encrypt address, and send the transaction with encrypted Username and encrypted Address to the Sputnik Blockchain. After it, Sputnik App has an information about the connection between the user’s [**Internal Address**](../glossary-of-terms-and-scheme/internal-address.md) and User's Username and/or Email&#x20;
* After this, Sputnik App shows the balance of Address to the [**User**](../glossary-of-terms-and-scheme/user.md) in the [**Personal Account**](personal-account.md)
